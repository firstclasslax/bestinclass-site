ABOUT

About Best
In Class

Best In Class is our vision of a valuable use of a high school recruit's time and energy. It's a 2-day training and recruiting event aimed to maximize both of those objectives. It's our first event that ties in a recruiting and evaluative component into the core player development sessions that FCL was built on.

Rising Juniors: This is an elite training event.

Rising Sophomores: Get better and burst onto the recruiting scene.

Rising Freshmen: Prepare for varsity and get your first elite training and showcase experience.

FAQs

Straight answers.

Do I need to apply to all events?

Summer invite-only events require an application. Open events (Summer Camp, Showcases) allow direct sign-up unless full. Players can apply here for invite-only events.

Can a coach nominate a player for BIC?

Yes. Coaches can submit nominations through our official nomination form. We review every submission. Nominations submitted outside this process aren't guaranteed acceptance.

Can I check my BIC application status?

We can't provide individual status updates. We receive thousands of applications. If you see the confirmation page after submitting, we've received your application. Email confirmation isn't needed. If you're unsure, you may resubmit.

What should I expect to hear back from Best In Class?

We send BIC invites out in waves. For Summer events, we typically stop sending invites out around December. We don't provide individual responses if players are not invited. We send an event status email to all applicants around December.
